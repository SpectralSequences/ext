express_basis

Given a vector elt, a subspace zeros of the total space (with a specified choice of complement) and a basis basis of a subspace of the complement, project elt to the complement and express as a linear combination of the basis. This assumes the projection of elt is indeed in the span of basis. The result is returned as a list of coefficients.
